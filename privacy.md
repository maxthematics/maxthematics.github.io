## 2. General

This website is a purely informational, **private** offering. **No cookies** are
set for analytics or advertising, **no tracking** and **no web analytics** (such
as Google Analytics) are used, and there is no comment function and no
advertising. Personal data is processed only to the technically necessary extent
described below.

## 3. Hosting

This website is hosted on **GitHub Pages**, a service provided by GitHub, Inc.,
based in the USA. When the pages are accessed, the host processes technically
necessary access data in server log files — in particular the IP address, the
date and time of access, the file requested, the volume of data transferred, and
browser and operating-system information. This processing is required for the
secure and stable operation of the website.

The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in the secure and
technically error-free provision of the website). As the provider is based in
the USA, data may be transferred there; this transfer is based on an adequacy
decision of the European Commission (EU-US Data Privacy Framework) or on
appropriate safeguards. Further information is available in GitHub's privacy
statement:
<https://docs.github.com/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement>.

## 4. External resources and embedded content

To display the website correctly, individual resources — such as icons and
components for rendering mathematical formulas — are loaded from external
**content delivery networks (CDNs)**. In addition, some pages embed interactive
graphics (applets) via an embedded frame (iframe). When this content is
retrieved, your device's IP address is transmitted to the respective provider,
as this is technically necessary to deliver the content to your browser;
individual providers may be based outside the EU.

The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in a functional and
appealing presentation of the website).

## 5. Contacting me

If you contact me by email, your details will be processed solely to handle your
enquiry. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR or, for contract-related matters,
Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR. The data will be deleted once it is no longer required for the
respective purpose and no statutory retention obligations apply.

## 6. Your rights

Subject to the statutory requirements, you have the right of access (Art. 15),
rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17), restriction of processing (Art. 18),
data portability (Art. 20), and to object to processing (Art. 21 GDPR). Please
contact the controller named above.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory
authority. The authority responsible for the controller is the **State
Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information North Rhine-Westphalia
(LDI NRW)**, <https://www.ldi.nrw.de>.
